what we do

EVERGREEN FLOWER

Original Floral Designs, Using A Wide Mix of Flowers. Each EVERGREEN floral arrangement will be a natural, original work of art. The company is committed to making each arrangement unique and custom-designed based on each customer’s needs. EVERGREEN'S floral arrangements will feature a wide range of seasonal flowers.

HOUSE DECORATION

Green and Flowering House Plants. EVERGREEN may occasionally offer a selection of seasonal green and flowering house plants. Paintings and Artwork from Local Artists. To create a gallery environment, EVERGREEN store will feature paintings and/or drawings from local artists, and the artwork will be for sale. The company will collect a small percentage of each sale (5%).

MARKETING

Free initial consulting. For retail and office tenants who are interested in a weekly delivery program, EVERGREEN will conduct an initial free, on-site visit to the customer’s office or store to determine the optimum floral design plan. Priority delivery program. EVERGREEN will work with each commercial customer to determine a weekly delivery schedule, and will assure that arrangements are delivered on the same day each week at the same time. Birthday Blooms. EVERGREEN offer a free arrangement to each office manager for a commercial account on his or her birthday.
